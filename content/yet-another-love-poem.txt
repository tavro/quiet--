i was so touched
by the softness of your eyes
i am so wrapped up in you
my thoughts drift up
away from words
i know my need of you more than before
i feel as though you must
you must be mine
i know that if i wait you will be mine
be it friendship or more
but i am unsure of where we are or where to go
but i know that dreams might still come true
though it may be i simply am a fool
i secretly watch you-
and patiently await my turn of acquaintance

i don't have much to say today
which isn't unusual
though it feels like i should apologize for it
like someone is waiting for me to produce a well-formed anecdote
but i don't have that
not because nothing has happened
things are always happening
too much, usually
the real reason i am quiet is this:
when i stop talking
something else starts
it is the sound of not having to explain yourself for a minute
of letting the day walk by
so i stay quiet
because i am listening for whatever it is that only shows up when i am not speaking
i think it might be the rest of me

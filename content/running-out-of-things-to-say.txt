we used to talk with sentences tumbling over each other
to feel close
now we sit on opposite ends of the couch
i ask how your day was
you ask what i am watching
remember when we couldn't shut up?
you cough
i sneeze
your knee turns slightly away from mine
i adjust the blanket
we talk logistics
soy milk,
rent,
who fed the birds?
i want to say something Big
"want some tea?"
"sure"
i bring you a cup
i place it near your hand
our fingers almost touch-
that is one kind of conversation

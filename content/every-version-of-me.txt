every version of me has been a bit of a disappointment
except the first one
that one had no expectations to live up to
i did not overthink things back then
i cried when things were bad
i have changed a lot since then
each new edition of me has quietly been throwing the old one under the bus
i keep thinking the next version will be better
the further i get from that first one
the more i miss how simple it was to just be
the next version will be kinder to me
maybe it will stop trying to be someone i would approve of
maybe the best version of me already happened?

open me someday
i whispered from the shelf
politely for attention
bright-eyed, well-meaning-- wearing a wool sweater
you bought me, remember?
you made promises about the kind of person you were becoming
it is not your fault the bird flew away
it is not your fault you had to stand in the rain for so long
it is not your fault you did not read me yet
no, truly, you did not mean it
so i tell myself

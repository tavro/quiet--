you did that thing with your eyes where they crinkle 
like they just heard the best joke
we walked three extra blocks because the conversation was so nice
you paused by a parked car to fix your hair in the side mirror
i paused too
pretended to check a text i didn't have
something about the act of looking down makes the heart feel safer
even if it is just an excuse to stand next to you without saying something stupid
i could have said
“you do that thing with your eyes”
but i didn't
because then it would have stopped being a thing you just do
and turn into a thing you would think about
and i like it better as the kind of thing you don't know you are doing
that accidentally breaks someone's heart in the nicest way possible

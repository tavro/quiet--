the right pronunciation of her name might unlock something
i once sent her a poem
and she replied that i should really write more
i have achieved literary obscurity
and the friend zone
i confess things like
you are kind of the point of everything right now
to which she responds
you are so sweet
which is the friendship version of
i love you
i believe in things
that one day she will text me and ask something like
have you ever thought about moving here?
and i will say
no, but i have already packed

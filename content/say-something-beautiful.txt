not sad, exactly
but remembering things i can not talk about
you and i sit on the couch
we have not said anything in over an hour
neither of us wants to take off first
your leg touches mine for a second
i think about asking something
and the silence is working harder
say something beautiful
you look at me like i have just handed you a dying bird
you do not say anything for a while
then finally
without looking up
"the light in here makes everything look gentle"
and for some reason i feel like crying

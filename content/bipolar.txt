i put confetti in my coffee.
that was fun.
and i loved the taste.
while drinking it i was applauding!
not metaphorically -- i was actually applauding!!!
now i am staring at the ceiling
with unnecessarily loud music playing in the background
laughing and crying at the same time
the confetti was a bad idea,
probably,
it is not charming anymore...
it looks like a clown had a nervous breakdown
it is on my table, my shirt...
my floor...
i don't have the energy to clean it up
i hate it!!

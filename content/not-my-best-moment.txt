it started with a joke
or what i thought was a joke
i laughed too hard at it
which is usually a sign it is not funny
i tried to walk it back
"wait, i did not mean that-
i meant it in a... conceptual way?"
a phrase i have never used before
and probably will not use again
you blinked a very slow blink
full of complete awareness of my bullshit
then, like a true professional
i said another thing
because when in doubt, add more gasoline
i stammered something that even i did not believe myself
and suddenly i could hear myself from the outside
if i could rewind, i would go back
to before my mouth decided to freelance
i am sorry, i truly am
even if this was not my best moment
i hope it is not our last one

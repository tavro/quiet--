i listen so hard, i forget to hear
i speak so fast, i cannot remember what i already said
people want clarity and it is always that thing that holds the whole thing
together
somehow
people stay, longer than expected
but i have not been touched in months
and before i am touched i need to grow--
now, when i realize this
i would like to be alone
maybe a long while
or
i need someone as cold and dark as me
